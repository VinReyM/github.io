Patricia C. Matias

As my Grandmother

She is my grandmother who lives in our province at Guinaoang, Mankayan, Benguet. We usually call her Lola taba or just lola, and she's fine with it. In a year, I always visit her twice or thrice since I currently live in Baguio City. And everytime I go home in our province, she always welcomes me with a warm hug. Oh how I miss those days when she gives me a big smile every time I visit her. It has not been long since she passed away and I always miss her every time I think about it. When I was a child, Lola would always let me take anything I want from her sari-sari store. She would always spoil me even if my parents are against it. During friday, we always go to the sayote farm to harvest some. She would teach me the proper way of harvesting vegetables and in return I help her carry all those sayote back home. It felt like it was just yesterday when we did it. She will always be the greatest Lola in my heart.

My message to her

Lola, I hope you are doing well where ever you are. You do not need to worry about me anymore, rest assured that I will always live my life to the fullest and finish my studies like you always say. It is unfortunate that you are not going to be there when I graduate. But I will always keep in mind everything you taught me and keep in my heart the love and care you gave me. I miss you Lola!
