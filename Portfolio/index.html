Webdesign

Creating and organizing content on a website, including visual and functional elements. It invovles planning, creating and arranging content to meet the needs of website owners and visitors, and is distinct from website development, which involves building and maintaining a website's structure.

Web Development

Process of creating, building and maintaining websites. It involves front end development whic deals with the user interface and back end development which handles server-side and database management. Web development requires technical skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities, and uses tools and technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and various frameworks and libraries.

Branding

Creating a unique and positive perception of a company or product in customers' minds by combining visual and messaging elements, and consistently applying them across all marketing channels to drive business growth and build trust and loyalty.

UI/UX Design

Process of creating user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing interfaces for digital prodcuts, combining visual design (UI) and user experience (UX) principles to ensure a seamless and enjoyable user experience.

Graphics Design
